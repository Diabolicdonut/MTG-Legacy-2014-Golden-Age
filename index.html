One thousand cards · Twenty historical waves

Compulsive Research

A preservation project devoted to the cards that shaped Magic’s competitive formats, deck-building language, design history, collecting culture, and shared player memory.

Entries 001–1000 20 illustrated waves Expanding honorable mentions Articles published within their waves
The purpose

Preserving the cards that made Magic

Compulsive Research gathers one thousand of the game’s most influential cards into a permanent historical archive. The collection spans tournament pillars, former stars, Commander institutions, design landmarks, cultural icons, notorious curiosities, and cards remembered simply because generations of players loved them.
