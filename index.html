Archive standard

One format for every deck

Each archetype can follow the same structure: identity, history, exact 75s, opening turns, interactions, piloting, sideboarding, matchup guide, historical notes, and a shared card pool capable of rebuilding the important variants.

The archive is intentionally organized as a playable museum rather than merely a collection of tournament lists.
